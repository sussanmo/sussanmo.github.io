Hi, I’m Suad!

I am currently a first-year PhD student at X University.

Before beginning my PhD, I worked as a Software Engineer at Microsoft under the Azure SDN team where I designed tools catered toward developer productivity, specifically in the DRI experience. I received my Bachelor’s degrees in Computer Science and Psychology at Belmont University.

My research interests include AI-assisted programming, computing education, and the cognitive implications of AI use in software development.

In my free time, I enjoy learning new languages, photography, and experimenting with coffee recipes. Feel free to contact me for collaborations and academic/mentorship opportunities.
